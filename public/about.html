Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Raddison Salon began as a small neighborhood beauty parlor with a big vision: to provide exceptional beauty services in a luxurious, welcoming environment. What started as a single-chair salon has now blossomed into one of the city's most sought-after beauty destinations.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, envisioned a space where clients could escape their busy lives and indulge in self-care. With a passion for beauty and a commitment to excellence, she carefully selected each team member who shares her vision of making every client feel special and beautiful.

Today, we continue to innovate and evolve, staying ahead of beauty trends while maintaining our core values of quality, professionalism, and personalized care. Every service we offer is backed by years of experience, ongoing training, and genuine passion for what we do.
